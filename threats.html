Threats faced by Turtles

Pollution, habitat loss, climate change, poaching, and fishing bycatch endanger turtles.

Pollution

Turtles are seriously threatened by pollution. Debris made of plastic can be mistaken for food, leading to internal injury and consumption. Turtles' bodies are covered with oil spills, affecting their ability to swim and regulate their body temperature. Chemical contaminants build up in their tissues, affecting their ability to reproduce and weakening their immune systems. The existence and health of these amazing species and their marine environments depend on pollution reduction.

Consequences

  • Hinder growth rate of turtles
  • Malnutrtion of turtles
  • Death of turtles

How to prevent the
Threat

  • Proper disposal of garbage
  • Avoid litering beaches
  • Recycling

Habitat loss

Turtles are significantly impacted by habitat loss. Due to coastal expansion, nesting beaches are being destroyed, which hinders successful reproduction. Their life cycles are disrupted by the loss of vital feeding grounds and nesting locations. Urbanization and deforestation cut down on the range of habitats. The survival of turtles and the preservation of the natural balance of our world depend on the conservation and restoration of their habitats.

Consequences

  • Affects life cycle of Turtles
  • Destruction of eggs
  • Turtle population depletion

How to prevent the
Threat

  • Have undisturebed land for Turtles
  • Be mindful when expanding marine structures
  • Increase number of turtle hatcheries

Illegal Harvesting

Turtles are seriously endangered by illegal harvesting. Populations are wiped off by poaching for their meat, shells, and eggs. They are taken advantage of by the exotic pet trade. Turtles are caught in illegal fishing nets as bycatch. To stop unlawful harvesting and guard against overuse and extinction, strict enforcement, education, and sustainable practices are essential.

Consequences

  • Increase in death of turtles
  • Affects marine life cycle
  • Threat of endangering of Turtles

How to prevent the
Threat

  • Raise awareness about illegal shell trade
  • Educating tourists and suppliers
  • Putting an end to illegal trading
