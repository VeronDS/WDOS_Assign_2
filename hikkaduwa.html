About Hikkaduwa...

In Hikkaduwa, Sri Lanka, there is a renowned center for turtle education and conservation known as the Hikkaduwa Turtle Hatchery. It is essential to the preservation and rehabilitation of sea turtles of all species, such as Olive Ridley, Green, Hawksbill, and Loggerhead. Eggs are collected and incubated, nests are protected, and hatchlings are released into the water at the hatchery. In addition to learning about the dangers confronting turtles and taking part in activities that support the hatchery's efforts, visitors may see the hatching process. It serves as a sign of optimism for the continued existence of these wonderful animals and their vulnerable marine environments.
