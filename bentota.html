About Bentota...

The Bentota Turtle Hatchery is a well-known establishment devoted to the preservation and conservation of sea turtles, and it is located in Bentota, Sri Lanka. The Olive Ridley, Green, Hawksbill, and Loggerhead turtles are just a few of the species being protected and nurtured by this hatchery. Collecting and safeguarding turtle eggs, guaranteeing their successful hatching, and releasing the hatchlings back into the water are all crucial tasks performed by the hatchery. Visitors can take part in informative tours, watch the hatching procedure, and help the conservation efforts of the hatchery by making donations. It acts as a vital refuge for the survival of these wonderful marine animals and promotes understanding of the value of turtle conservation.
